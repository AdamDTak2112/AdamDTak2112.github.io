# About Adam

I originally hail from Idaho Falls, Idaho where I grew up through most of my life. I am currently attending Utah State University where I study Computer Science and Philosophy.

## Why Computer Science?

Ever since I was a child, I loved technology. We had a computer in the basement that I would spend many-a-night playing old DOS games on, like Privateer, Oregon Trail, and Where in the World is Carmen San Diego. Naturally, once I came to school at Utah State I fell further in love with the school of computer science. The idea of creating something both entirely abstract as well as entirely functional fulfills both my passion for creativity as well as my respect for the function of logic in our real world.
